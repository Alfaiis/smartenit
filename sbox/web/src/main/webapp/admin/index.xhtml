Welcome to SBox

The SBox is the central entity of the SmartenIT architecture, hosting the Dynamic Traffic Management mechanism.

You will be able to configure relevant ISP, network, datacenters and SBox parameters.

Learn more

ISP

Configure general ISP information and Autonomous systems.

Network

Configure the ISP's internal network entities and parameters. Specifically, provide information for the SDN controllers, BG and DA routers, links and tunnels.

Datacenters

Configure local and remote clouds, and all inter-datacenter communication sets.

SBox

Configure SBox parameters, get reports and analytics, as well as navigate to the demonstration page.
